Books that have shaped my thinking on technology, business, and life

Books I Have Read

Zero to One: Notes on Startups, Or How to Build the Future

by Blake Masters and Peter Thiel

A guide to building innovative companies that create new value rather than competing in existing markets, emphasizing the importance of monopolies and thinking differently about the future.

The Hard Thing About Hard Things

by Ben Horowitz

Practical wisdom on navigating the toughest challenges of running a startup, from making difficult decisions to managing company culture during crisis.

Atomic Habits: An Easy & Proven Way to Build Good Habits & Break Bad Ones

by James Clear

A comprehensive framework for understanding how habits work and practical strategies for building better habits through small, incremental changes.
